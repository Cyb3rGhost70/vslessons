WyCo Resilency

The mission of our Mutual Assistance Group and Team is to educate and promote individual, family, and Community preparedness for disasters. To educate, provide planning, training, and resource options to the MAG for preparations as well as response to a worst case, Short/Mid/Long term catastrophe affecting the community. To assist one another, and the community to the best of our abilities. Extremis Malis Extrema Remedia Exodus 15.3
